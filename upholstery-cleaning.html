EXPERT UPHOLSTERY CLEANING BY FANTASTIC CLEANERS

You need professional upholstery cleaning in Swansea? You want a truly gleaming service service delivered straight to your door?

Choose professional upholstery cleaning by GWA's Carpet Cleaners and book truly professional results! We are proud to deliver outstanding customer satisfaction and excellent services.

GWA's specialises in professional cleaning services for the wide range of upholstery fabrics, such as silk, Haitian cotton, velour, brushed corduroy, leather, non-colourfast fabrics, fabrics with cleaning code S or Y and more. We clean sofas, chairs, curtains, drapery and anything upholstered by using the latest methods and technology.

What's included in professional upholstery cleaning service?

  • Pre-treating the fabric;
  • Stain and spot treatment;
  • Steam cleaning (if applicable);
  • Dry cleaning via foam or solvent;
  • Extraction of stains and dirt;
  • Deodorising your upholstery;
  • Results: fresh and spotless clean furniture;
  • Less hassle and more free time for you.

What types of upholstery cleaning do you provide?

  • Steam Cleaning - when applicable;
  • Dry Foam Cleaning - for delicate fabrics;
  • Leather Cleaning - via dry solvents.

In addition, we can somewhat "repair" leather upholstery with special stain removers and crack fillers. However, you have to first send us pictures of your furnishing so we can determine what kind of solutions will work the best.

The Scotchgard™ upholstery treatment is the best (upon request)

For both new furnishing and old - the Scotchgard™ upholstery treatment will protect your fabric from a wide range of furniture stains: ink, red wine, shoe polisher, blood, crayons and more.

* NOTE: 1) We might not clean mat leather as it is usually not protected with a rejuvenating solution, thus it wears down pretty quickly. If you book cleaning of mat leather, its colour could change.
