What is included in your private/thorough house cleaning service?

  • Clean all Bedrooms: dust reachable surfaces, change bedding, polish mirrors, empty bins/change bin bags, hoover and/or mop the floor, wipe all skirtings.
  • Sanitise Bathrooms: sanitise all reachable surfaces, clean the shower cabin and/or bathtub, sanitise the toilet, clean and polish mirrors, mop the floor, empty bins/change bin bags.
  • Disinfect the Kitchen: wipe and sanitise all surfaces, clean hobs/splash-back, clean microwave, vacuum and/or mop floor, empty bins/change bin bags, clean all skirtings. Please mind that dip-tank oven valeting is not included in the service, but is available upon request for an additional charge.
  • Dust off your Living room: dust/polish all reachable surfaces, wipe all furniture, empty bins/change bin bags, hoover and/or mop the floor, clean skirtings.
  • Wipe Hallways & Staircases: dust off reachable surfaces, wipe skirting boards, wipe bannisters, hoover and/or mop the floor.

Upon request: clean windows from within, fold/organise clothes in wardrobes, water plants, feed your cat/dog, do laundry, hang laundry and even do the ironing.
